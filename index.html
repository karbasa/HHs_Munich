What is Hacks/Hackers?


Hacks/Hackers is a global community at the intersection of journalism & technology. Learn more about H/Hs.


What do we do?


We meet once every 6 weeks in Munich to discuss the hottest trends and best practices and do hands-on stuff as well.


Who can participate?


Journalists, newsroom developers, designers and basically everyone interested in journalism & technology is welcome!
